Design Environment

Patient Care Design is an ideology that begins with the assumption that Health Care Providers (HCPs) should spend their valuable time with their patients, and in collaborative, rapid, reflective problem solving. Minimal time should be spent managing extraneous processes (such as recording documentation, manual calculation, and order entry). In doing so, HCPs become empowered to spend more time in the construction of high quality patient care planning. Central to this endeavour is “lifting” HCPs into the realm of design tools more familiar to architects, and engineers
